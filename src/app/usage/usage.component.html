Courses

The Courses section is the fun part, here you can keep track of all your marks and see your progress in the course.

To do so, create a course, there is an example course Physics with a couple of marks so you can play around.

To edit a course title, or code click the name you'd like to edit, it will switch to an input field this is where you can edit. Similar to this, you can edit any field of a mark other than its contribution.
